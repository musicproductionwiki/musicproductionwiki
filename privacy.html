Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

MusicProductionWiki.com ("MPW", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

We collect limited data to improve the site and understand how producers use MPW:

  • Usage data — pages visited, time on site, referrer URLs, device type. Collected via Google Analytics 4 (GA4).
  • Newsletter data — if you subscribe to The Producer's Briefing, we collect your email address via Beehiiv.
  • No account data — MPW does not require registration or store personal accounts.

2. Cookies

We use cookies for analytics only (Google Analytics). No advertising cookies are used. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

3. Google Analytics

MPW uses Google Analytics 4 to understand site traffic. Google may collect anonymized usage data per their Privacy Policy. IP addresses are anonymized. We do not share this data with third parties.

4. Newsletter (Beehiiv)

If you subscribe to The Producer's Briefing, your email is stored by Beehiiv per their Privacy Policy. You can unsubscribe at any time via the link in any email.

5. Affiliate Links

MPW does not currently use affiliate links or earn affiliate commissions. If we add affiliate partnerships in future, they will be disclosed on our Affiliate Disclosure page, and affiliate partners may then set cookies to track referrals per their own privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by law or as described in this policy (analytics providers, newsletter platform).

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold. Contact us at team@musicproductionwiki.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Material changes will be noted with an updated date at the top of this page.

9. Contact

Questions? Email team@musicproductionwiki.com
