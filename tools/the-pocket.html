Why this is honest, not a horoscope

Every browser adds a fixed delay between your tap and the clock, and so does your own reaction time — which would label everyone a laid-back genius. So Round 1 measures your personal zero on a bare metronome, and Round 2 subtracts it. What's left is real: how far your feel drifts once you stop concentrating. The timeline shows every tap scattered ahead of or behind the beat, so you can see the result for yourself.
