Free Music Production Tools — Built for Real Sessions

Every tool in this hub started as a real problem in a real session. The Loudness Penalty Calculator exists because mastering engineers kept submitting hotter masters that got turned down by streaming platforms. The Frequency Conflict Detector exists because mixing engineers needed to visualize masking, not just guess at it.

These are free music production tools for producers at every level. No account required. No paywalled basics. The tool runs in your browser and works on mobile. Calculations use real math — not lookup tables dressed up as calculators.

More tools ship regularly. The full suite covers every stage of the production process: mixing, mastering, sound design, arrangement, and music business. Bookmark this page and check back.
