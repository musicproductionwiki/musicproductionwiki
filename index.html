Quick Glossary

Full glossary →
LUFS
Loudness Units relative to Full Scale
Sidechain
Routing signal to control another processor
Transient
Initial attack peak of a sound
Headroom
Space between peak signal and 0 dBFS
Saturation
Harmonic distortion that adds warmth
Stem
Grouped export of instrument layers
Dry/Wet
Ratio of unprocessed to processed signal
Bit Depth
Dynamic range resolution of digital audio
